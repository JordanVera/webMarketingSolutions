Our SMM Process

Our 4 step SMM process ensures that your business gets the most out of our services.

  • Discovery
  • Strategy
  • Campaign Execution
  • Analysis & Reporting

Discover Opportunities

The discovery stage is where we will learn everything we can about your company and its past performance on social media (if you have any). Upon auditing your current social media campaigns, we will begin to develop the campaign strategy. We may recommend hopping on new platforms during this stage is we think it will benefit your company.

  • Project brief: Define client information & business goals
  • Audience Research: Use ad tools to identify audience.
  • Competitive Analaysis: Define competitors in SMM platforms.
  • Social Audit: Create baseline for current social performance.
